About Me


Hi! I'm Leigh, a yoga teacher, writer, interdisciplinary theatre artist, and burgeoning web developer from Austin, TX. Working in the theater world for nearly 30 years (I started VERY young, stop trying to do the math) has given me a keen eye for design, and the ability to make somthing really cool with virtually no time and even less money.


I started using my writing and design skills to create Wellsites™ (health and wellness focused websites) in WordPress. When I learned that a writer and designer who can also code was called a "Unicorn" I immediately knew that was what I wanted to be when I grew up. And here we are.


I also quite likes punching things, and am working towards my orange belt in Krav Maga. So it's probalby a good idea to just smile and go along with it. Whatever it is. But don't worry, a warrior never starts fights, she only finishes them. #SoThatOneMayWalkInPeace
